Style Stories

New Summer Dress Collection

Mon. May 25th 2025

Our new summer dress collection is all about movement, light fabrics, and soft pastel tones. Each piece is designed to feel effortless, whether you are heading to a brunch, a beach walk, or a late-night gathering with friends.

We focused on breathable materials, flattering cuts, and delicate details like ruffles, tie-straps, and subtle prints, so you can mix and match your look without losing comfort or elegance.

Everyday Styling Tips

Thu. June 5th 2025

Building an everyday outfit doesn’t have to be complicated. Start with simple basics like a plain top and high-waist jeans, then add one statement piece such as a printed blazer, a bold bag, or layered necklaces.

In this style story we share easy combinations, color ideas, and small tweaks that turn a normal look into something confident and polished without spending hours in front of the mirror.

Fabric & Quality Story

Sun. July 12th 2025

Each Lunara piece starts with fabric. We carefully choose textures that drape well, feel soft on the skin, and last through everyday wear. From lightweight cottons to structured crepe, every material is tested for comfort and durability.

This feature takes you behind the scenes of how we source our fabrics, work with trusted suppliers, and refine every detail to make sure your favorite items stay in your wardrobe for seasons, not just weeks.

Behind The Scenes Photoshoot

Our campaign shoots are where the collection comes to life. From choosing the location and moodboard to styling the models, every detail is planned to reflect the brand’s personality and the story of the season.

Take a look at candid moments, outfit changes, and the creative energy behind the camera, and discover how a simple sketch turns into the final image you see on the website and social media.
